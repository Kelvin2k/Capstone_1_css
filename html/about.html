Our history of Pooch Care

Our History

At Pooch Care, we began our journey in 2017 with a passion for providing exceptional care for pets. In 2018, we expanded our services to include grooming and training. By 2019, we had grown our team and opened a second location. In 2020, we introduced a new line of organic pet food to support healthier lifestyles for our furry friends.
